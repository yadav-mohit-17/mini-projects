

Q) What is Minutes of Meeting(MoM)?
-> A Minutes of Meeting (MoM) is a written document that summarizes the key discussions, decisions, and action items from a meeting. Its primary purpose is to provide a clear and concise record of what was discussed and agreed upon during the meeting.


SAMPLE EMAIL OF MOM (MINUTES OF MEETING)
---------------------------------------------

Subject: Minutes of Meeting - [Meeting Date]

Dear [Recipient's Name],

I hope this email finds you well. Please find attached the Minutes of Meeting (MoM) for our recent discussion held on [Meeting Date]. The meeting was productive, and we covered the following agenda items:

Meeting Details:

Date: [Meeting Date]
Time: [Meeting Time]
Location/Platform: [Physical location or virtual platform used]
Attendees:

List of participants present: [List names]
Agenda Items Discussed:

[Brief summary of each agenda item discussed]
Decisions Made:

[List of decisions made during the meeting]
Action Items:

[List of action items assigned to each participant]
Deadlines and responsibilities outlined for each action item.
Next Steps:

[Summary of next steps or follow-up actions]
Please review the attached MoM for accuracy and completeness. If you have any corrections or additional points to add, kindly let me know by [Specify deadline for feedback, if any]. Your prompt review and feedback are appreciated.

Thank you for your attention to this matter. Should you have any questions or require further clarification on any of the discussed points, please don't hesitate to reach out.

Best regards,

[Your Full Name]
[Your Position]
[Your Contact Information]

Attachments:

Minutes of Meeting (MoM) - [Meeting Date]
